About Us

Filipino food is a mixture of sweet, salty and sour flavors. Most Filipino dishes are heavy on meat like beef (baka), chicken (manok), pork (baboy), and ocassionally goat (kambing). Offal is also commonly eaten by Filipinos and forms the main ingredient of dishes like bopis, la paz bachoy and sisig.
